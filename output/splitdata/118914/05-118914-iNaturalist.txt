"Echinargus isola","2016-05-22 00:00:00 -0600","Reakirt's Blue (Echinargus isola) - female

22 May 2016
Ray Roberts Lake State Park, Isle Du Bois
Pilot Point, Denton County, Texas

A single somewhat worn female Reakirt's Blue was feeding on the Texas Vervain alongside the trail. Reakirt's Blue's range extends from ""southern California, Arizona, New Mexico and Texas [thence] through Mexico and Central America to Costa Rica. [It is a] summer migrant north and east through the Mississippi River states to Wisconsin and Ohio."" Its northward flight in the United States is known to take it as far to the northwest as Washington and Montana. It flies throughout the year in South Texas. A very small butterfly, Reakirt's Blue is primarily identified with the United States and Mexico. Because of its extensive flight in the Americas and North and Central America in particular, Reakirt's Blue is an authentic resident of the Western Hemisphere. Ray Roberts Lake State Park, Isle Du Bois, is administered by the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department.
