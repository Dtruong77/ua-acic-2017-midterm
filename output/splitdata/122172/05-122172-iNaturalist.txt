Ray Roberts Lake State Park, Isle Du Bois
Pilot Point, Denton County, Texas

Little Yellow has an extensive range that encompasses Central America north to Mexico and thence to the southern United States as indicated at the link for this species at BugGuide. Little Yellow's range in the United States ""represents seasonal colonization from further south."" Little Yellow's other common names include Little Sulphur and Little Sulfur. Its wingspan varies between 32-44 mm. Because of its extensive range in Central and North America, Little Yellow is an authentic resident of the Western Hemisphere. Ray Roberts Lake State Park, Isle Du Bois, is administered by the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department.
